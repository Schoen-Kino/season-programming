Season 8: Grizzly Man

It's not nice to eat your friends…! Try telling that to a bear. Grizzly Man is a tragicomedy documentary of Timothy Treadwell, an untrained film-maker who believed had a special connection to bears in Alaska. Until one day he is eaten by one. Herzog takes hundreds of hours of videotape and assembles them into a story: sublime, dramatic shots of the bears, teaching us about nature and… well… human stupidity. In line with current world news, here's to a bizarre start of the year!
